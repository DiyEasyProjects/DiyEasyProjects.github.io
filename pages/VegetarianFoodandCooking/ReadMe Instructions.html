Sales Page Edit Instructions

Please make sure you follow these instructions to get the most out of your PLR Monthly Minisite Page.

1. Navigate inside the folder of the minisite sales page that you wish to use, open the page index.html in your favorite HTML editor, you can even use a simple text editor like notepad if you wish.

2. Find the text:
<form><input src="images/buy.jpg" name="submit" border="0" type="image"></form>
and replace it with your own Paypal payment button.
To create your own button in Paypal, simply login and click on the 'Merchant Services' tab, then click on the 'sell single items' link to create your own payment button.

3. Upload the contents of the folder to your web host, in your required location.
